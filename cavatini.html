Cavatini

Description

A delicious type of pasta that is sure to satiate even the pickiest eater! This recipe calls for pasta sauce of your choice, Rotini noodles and, finally, some ground beef. make sure to cook gound beef before mixing with pasta.

From here we will pre-heat the oven to 450 degrees farenheight and bake the pasta for 20 minutes.

  • 2 cans of pasta sauce
  • Rotini pasta noodle
  • Ground beef

Cooking Steps

  1. cook Ground beef in pot until visibly brown.
  2. mix pasta sauce with Rotini noodles while beef cooks.
  3. once beef is cooked mix all ingredients in a pasta plate platter of your choice.
  4. toss into oven for 20 minutes at 450 degrees farenheight.
  5. Enjoy!
